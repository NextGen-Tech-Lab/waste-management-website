# Quick Start: Education Video Upload

## For Admins - 5 Minute Setup Guide

### Step 1: Log In
- Go to Admin Dashboard
- Ensure you're logged in as admin

### Step 2: Open Education Panel
- Click the **"Education"** button in the top navigation
- You'll see the education management interface

### Step 3: Add Your First Video
1. Click **"+ Add YouTube Video"** button
2. Enter **Video Title** (e.g., "How to Recycle Plastic")
3. Enter **Description** (e.g., "Learn proper plastic recycling methods")
4. Select **Category** from dropdown
5. Paste **YouTube URL** (any format works):
   - Full Link: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
   - Short Link: `https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID`
   - Just ID: `VIDEO_ID`
6. See **Preview** of embedded video
7. Click **"Add Video"** → Video saved as DRAFT

### Step 4: Publish Video
- Click the **Publish icon** (↑) next to the video
- Video now appears to users!

---

## For Users - Finding Videos

1. Go to **Education Center** page
2. See 5 category cards with video counts
3. Click on category you're interested in
4. Watch the embedded YouTube video
5. Use Next/Previous buttons to browse videos
6. Like videos you find helpful

## Admin Common Tasks

| Task | Steps |
|------|-------|
| **Add Video** | Click "+ Add YouTube Video" → Fill form → Click "Add Video" |
| **Edit Video** | Click pencil icon → Modify details → Click "Update Video" |
| **Publish Video** | Click arrow up icon next to draft video |
| **Unpublish Video** | Click arrow down icon next to published video |
| **Delete Video** | Click trash icon → Confirm |
| **View Stats** | Cards at top show Total / Published / Draft |

## YouTube URL Examples

All these work:
```
✓ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
✓ https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ
✓ https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ
✓ dQw4w9WgXcQ
```

Where to get URL:
1. Open video on YouTube
2. Click **Share** button
3. Copy the link
4. Paste in admin form
